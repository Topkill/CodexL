# CodexL

CodexL is the all-in-one launcher and enhancement suite for Codex App. It turns Codex into a multi-workspace command center where you can launch faster, keep projects isolated, manage provider and proxy profiles, control Codex from your phone, and extend your AI workflow with built-in gateway power.

中文文档见 [README_zh.md](README_zh.md).

Detailed documentation: [https://codexl.io/docs](https://codexl.io/docs).

## Why CodexL

CodexL gives developers a focused control center for Codex App. Every project can have its own workspace profile, model-provider setup, proxy route, and remote-control access, all managed from one polished desktop cockpit.

Use CodexL to remove config juggling, protect workspace boundaries, switch model providers with confidence, and keep Codex reachable from your phone when you step away from your desk.

## Project Highlights

- One launcher for every Codex workspace, with clean project separation and faster context switching.
- One-click launch control with the right provider, proxy, language, and workspace profile already in place.
- Phone-ready remote control for keeping Codex sessions moving when you step away from your desk.
- Built-in Bot Gateway and NeXT AI Gateway extensions to connect Codex with IM platforms and flexible provider workflows.
- A mobile control experience that opens from a quick scan or session link.

## Built for High-Velocity AI Work

CodexL helps you keep multiple Codex environments ready for different clients, projects, models, and network routes. Switch context without rebuilding your setup from scratch, and keep each workspace clean enough for serious daily use.

## Remote Control That Follows You

Start a Codex session on desktop, then keep an eye on it from your phone. CodexL makes remote control feel natural for long-running tasks, quick checks, and lightweight handoffs when you are away from your keyboard.

## Gateway Extensions Included

CodexL ships with built-in gateway extensions designed to make Codex more connected. Bring Codex closer to IM platforms, flexible provider workflows, and custom AI routing without turning every setup into a manual integration project.

## Who It Is For

- Developers running several Codex projects at the same time.
- Builders who switch between providers, proxies, and workspace profiles.
- Teams that want cleaner Codex workflows across desktop, mobile, and gateway-powered integrations.
